Support the Project

I know how most of you treat this world-building thing more like a hobby, without any intention for your work to be known outside of your circle. People have different goals in life. Well, my goal is to make my work something real. I’m relentless in my efforts, trying to figure it all out. I’ll make it happen. That’s my goal. It always has been.

So yeah, if that’s not yours… let me represent you. If that is your goal, I hope to inspire you in the same way you inspired me. Let me cook the coolest story ever told. I’ve been taking a little piece of every single one of you, connecting us all in the threads of the Aetherway . That’s what this project represents.

I need your help in any way you can:

  • Sharing the project with someone who might enjoy it
  • Giving feedback – help me grow
  • Contribute to the gallery – draw my characters and get featured!
  • buying Aetherway’s merch : t-shirts and hand-made stickers featuring the characters and illustrations from the stories.
  • donating or becoming a member on my Ko-fi : get early access and doodles of your OC each month!
