Apartment Dimension 7 - Dinner Party

INT. KITCHEN – EVENING

The kitchen is bustling with the chaos of preparing for a dinner party. Fire is trying to keep things simple, while Millennium Whoop, Lady Thread, and Lady Entanglement offer increasingly bizarre suggestions.

FIRE:
Okay, I’m just gonna keep it simple. Spaghetti, garlic bread, salad. That’s it.

LADY ENTANGLEMENT (looking over her shoulder):
Spaghetti? That is an inefficient carbohydrate delivery system. I suggest Nebula Pasta. It uses gravitational forces to keep the sauce perfectly aligned with the noodles.

FIRE:
No! No nebula anything. Just… spaghetti.

CONFLICT:
Everyone keeps complicating the meal with their strange suggestions, and Fire gets more frustrated trying to keep things normal.

LADY THREAD:
Oh no! I see a future where someone spills the sauce and it triggers a sauce-dimensional slipstream! What if we just serve anti-spaghetti? It’s like regular spaghetti, but it exists in the space between bites.

FIRE:
That’s not even food, Lady Thread!

MILLENNIUM WHOOP (spinning, voice crackling):
Why no K-3 pizza? Many zippity zaps, no tummy boom-boom! Whoop whoop!

FIRE:
Because… humans don’t eat extra-dimensional pizza. It gives us heartburn… in every dimension.

RESOLUTION:
After all the confusion, the neighbors arrive, and it turns out they don’t even like spaghetti, so the whole fuss was for nothing.

NEIGHBOR:
Oh, we’re actually gluten-free. Do you have… salad?

FIRE (looking at the group, exhausted):
You know what? We’ve got something from another dimension that might be perfect for you.

TAG:
Fire, resigned to the multiverse of culinary madness, eyes a mysterious jar labeled “Interdimensional Lettuce” with dread.

FADE OUT.
