Apartment Dimension 8 - TV remote

INT. LIVING ROOM – EVENING

The living room is filled with the chaos of arguing over the TV remote. Fire, Millennium Whoop, Lady Thread, and Lady Entanglement are all battling for control, each wanting to watch something different.

FIRE:
Can we just pick something and stick with it? You guys keep changing the channel, and now I’m getting a headache from flipping between dimensions.

MILLENNIUM WHOOP (grabbing the remote):
Noooo, prank show! Prank-prank, BOOM! Pop—zapzapzap… Speed dial chaos!

LADY ENTANGLEMENT:
Reality shows are a waste of temporal energy. We should tune into Dimension 1’s Historical Documentaries on the rise of logic-based civilizations.

FIRE:
Lady Entanglement, no offense, but watching logic civilizations rise is like watching paint dry—without the paint.

CONFLICT:
They keep flipping between increasingly bizarre shows from different dimensions, and Fire just wants to watch something familiar.

LADY THREAD:
There’s a 43% chance that if we watch the drama on Dimension 4, it’ll mirror our future exactly… which might help us make better decisions!

FIRE:
I don’t need interdimensional life advice! I just want to relax and watch Seinfeld reruns!

MILLENNIUM WHOOP:
Nothing show? Boooo! Watch EVERYTHING SHOW! Full-on cosmic buffet, munch munch!

RESOLUTION:
After an argument, Fire finally gets to put on Seinfeld, but then the interdimensional signal cuts out, leaving them with no TV at all.

FIRE:
I don’t care what dimension it’s from, I just want one normal channel!

(The TV fuzzes out, and everyone sits in silence, staring at the blank screen.)

LADY ENTANGLEMENT:
The absence of entertainment is the most logical outcome.

TAG:

The silence is deafening as they all sit, still holding the remote, contemplating the void of interdimensional TV.

FADE OUT.
