Apartment Dimension 3 - Neighbor

INT. FRONT DOOR – DAY

Fire stands nervously in front of the door, glancing at the interdimensional roommates as they prepare to meet their new neighbor. The atmosphere is tense with excitement.

FIRE:
Okay, guys. We have a new neighbor coming over. Please, please try to act normal!

MILLENNIUM WHOOP (currently a neon-colored parrot perched on Fire’s shoulder):
Define normal? Zig-zag, loopity-loop, straight-line wobble? Me circuits say: normal just laundry machine setting! Whoosh!

LADY ENTANGLEMENT (on her computer):
My research shows that ’normal’ is an extremely inefficient way of existing.

LADY THREAD:
Oh no! I foresee an awkward interaction where he’ll realize we’re not from this dimension! There’s a 90% chance he’ll run away screaming!

FIRE:
Not helping, Lady Thread!

(The doorbell rings. Fire nervously opens it to reveal a cheerful neighbor holding cookies.)

NEIGHBOR:
Hi! I’m Steve from next door! Thought I’d welcome you with some cookies!

FIRE:
Oh, thanks! I—uh—love cookies. So, um, yeah, everything’s totally normal in here.

(Behind Fire, Millennium Whoop morphs into a floating cat, then a lamp, and then a giant ice cream cone.)

STEVE:
Did that lamp just…?

FIRE:
Nope! Totally normal lamp. Ha ha… nothing weird here!

LADY THREAD (appearing suddenly next to Steve, whispering):
You should probably leave before the space-time continuum unravels. I’ve seen it happen in 4 out of 7 timelines.

STEVE:
Excuse me?!

RESOLUTION:
Fire manages to convince Steve that everything is fine, just before Lady Entanglement inadvertently makes it worse.

LADY ENTANGLEMENT (approaching the door):
Human Steve, we have heard rumors of a ‘block party.’ Does this event involve multi-dimensional convergence, or is it strictly carbon-based beings?

STEVE (nervously):
Uh… just, you know, normal stuff. Burgers and chips.

FIRE (smiling awkwardly):
See? Nothing weird here at all!

(The door closes. Steve leaves, looking thoroughly confused. Fire sighs in relief. The moment the door closes, Millennium Whoop turns into a balloon and pops.)

FADE OUT.
