Apartment Dimension 2 - Groceries

INT. KITCHEN – DAY

A cluttered kitchen filled with strange foods and gadgets from different dimensions. The atmosphere is chaotic, with floating cans, glowing fruit, and shelves that defy conventional physics.

FIRE:
Alright, guys, we need to get some groceries. What do we need?

LADY ENTANGLEMENT (without looking up from her laptop):
We require 7 liters of antimatter milk. It’s the only substance capable of neutralizing temporal anomalies in cereal.

FIRE:
Uh, no antimatter. The last time we got it, it vaporized the fridge.

LADY THREAD:
Oh! We should definitely get some Quantum Eggs! But… oh no, there’s a 25% chance they’ll hatch into sentient beings that could alter the timeline…

FIRE:
We’re sticking with regular eggs.

MILLENNIUM WHOOP (appears out of nowhere in the form of a flying fish):
Whoop whoop! Transdimensional Bananas? Peely-wheely wibbly-wobbly snack attack! You chomp—uh-oh—it chomp-chomps back! Zippy zappy fun time!

FIRE (exasperated):
No! Just… no.

LADY ENTANGLEMENT:
You are being irrational. Without Void Butter, we risk entropy seepage in all sandwich-based meals.

FIRE:
What even is Void Butter?! And why does everything have to risk destroying the universe?!

LADY THREAD (looking into the distance):
I see a future where we run out of coffee… It’s a bleak, terrible place.

FIRE:
Okay, coffee. That’s reasonable. But just coffee. None of this ‘dark matter creamer’ nonsense!

MILLENNIUM WHOOP (turning into a giant spoon):
Whoop whoop! Extra spoons, always! More spoons, more swooshy-scoopy cosmic chaos! Never too many—INFINITE SPOON MODE!

FIRE (looking frustrated but trying to remain calm):
Alright. We’re done. Eggs, bread, coffee… normal stuff.

LADY ENTANGLEMENT:
Indeed. Also, 14 kilograms of hypercube potatoes. They fold neatly in the pantry.

FIRE (defeated):
Fine. Hypercube potatoes.

FADE OUT.
