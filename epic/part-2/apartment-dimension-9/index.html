Apartment Dimension 9 - Laundry

INT. LAUNDRY ROOM – DAY

Fire is in the laundry room, sorting through a pile of clothes. Millennium Whoop and Lady Thread walk in, each with an outlandish understanding of how laundry works.

FIRE:
Alright, guys, laundry day. Who’s first?

MILLENNIUM WHOOP:
Laundry? Me go poof new clothes whoosh!

LADY ENTANGLEMENT:
I use dimensional fabric that cleans itself when exposed to high-frequency thought waves. Laundry is illogical.

FIRE:
Well, I can’t thought-wash my socks, so into the washer they go.

CONFLICT:
They try to “help” Fire with the laundry, but only make things worse by suggesting strange methods of interdimensional cleaning.

MILLENNIUM WHOOP (gesturing to the washing machine):
This? Pfft… time-vortex! Clothes zap, dirt whoosh, clean before dirty!

FIRE:
No. No messing with time. I don’t need to be walking around with socks that exist outside of linear reality.

LADY ENTANGLEMENT:
If we quantum-fold the clothes, they would take up zero space in your dresser. Infinite storage.

FIRE:
I just need clean socks!

RESOLUTION:
After refusing all their weird suggestions, Fire finishes his laundry, only to realize that the spin cycle has somehow mixed his socks with tiny interdimensional portals.

FIRE (pulling out socks):
Why do all my socks have tiny black holes in them?

MILLENNIUM WHOOP:
Z-socks! Holes tiny, thoughts BIG. No blink too hard… Blink back reaction!

TAG:

The socks continue to hum with a strange energy as Fire stares at them, contemplating if he should risk it.

FADE OUT.
