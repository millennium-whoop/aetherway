Apartment Dimension 5 - Couch

INT. LIVING ROOM – DAY

The living room is the same as always, but Fire is sitting uncomfortably on the couch, looking frustrated. Millennium Whoop, Lady Thread, and Lady Entanglement are around, watching.

FIRE (sitting uncomfortably):
Is it just me, or is the couch weird?

MILLENNIUM WHOOP:
Weird? Nahhh, it’s same-same… Mostly! Maybe couch go little wobbly-wobble today? Whoop whoop!

FIRE:
Mostly? What do you mean mostly?

MILLENNIUM WHOOP (smirking):
Okay-okay! Me maybe swap—couch from Dimension 7X-14… little wibbly-wobbly, little whooshy-floaty! Whoop whoop!

FIRE:
Why?! What’s wrong with our couch?

MILLENNIUM WHOOP:
Nothing! This one… 0.04% MORE couch! Whoop whoop! Squish-squish ZAP-ZAP! Extra comfy-float!

CONFLICT:
Fire insists on getting the original couch back, but the others don’t see the big deal. Lady Thread is especially uninterested, and Lady Entanglement is looking at the couch like it’s an alien artifact.

FIRE:
It’s not right! It’s too… squishy in the middle, and the cushions don’t feel like they’re attached to reality. I can’t get comfortable!

LADY ENTANGLEMENT (deadpan, behind her laptop):
Comfort is a subjective concept. If the couch maintains structural integrity, there is no issue.

FIRE:
Oh, there’s an issue. I sat down and now I feel like I’m leaning into a wormhole.

MILLENNIUM WHOOP:
Best part! Sit on cloud… but cloud’s like… ‘what’s gravity?’ Floaty-wobbly, squish-squish!

RESOLUTION:
Fire insists they get the old couch back, but when Millennium Whoop finally brings it back, Fire realizes it was just their mood all along, and the original couch feels just as weird.

FIRE (after sitting on the “old” couch again):
Wait… this feels just like the other one.

MILLENNIUM WHOOP (laughing, circuits flickering): Heehee! It is! Me never swap-back! Whoop whoop! Sneaky couch-trickery ZAP!

FIRE (defeated):
I’m getting my own chair.

TAG:
The couch remains a weird, interdimensional mystery, and Fire retreats to the corner of the room, contemplating the strange nature of furniture in a multiverse.

FADE OUT.
