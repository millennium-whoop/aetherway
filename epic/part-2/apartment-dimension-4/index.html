Apartment Dimension 4 - Wi-fi

INT. LIVING ROOM – DAY

The living room is in chaos. Fire is on the phone with the cable company, while the interdimensional ladies react wildly to the lack of internet.

FIRE (frustrated):
Yes, I’ve restarted the router! Still no signal. Can you just send a technician?

LADY ENTANGLEMENT:
Our connection to the Aetherway Data Stream has been severed. This must be rectified immediately, or we may lose access to crucial multi-dimensional data.

FIRE:
Yeah, and I can’t stream Netflix.

MILLENNIUM WHOOP (flickering between shapes, jittery):
Whoop whoop! No Wi-Fi?! Me circuits MELTDOWN! Need zippy-zappy chaos memes from 6th dimension—EMERGENCY!

LADY THREAD:
Oh no… No internet means we’re cut off from the future… What if the future changes while we’re offline?!

CONFLICT:
The beings try to “help” fix the Wi-Fi, leading to even more chaos.

LADY ENTANGLEMENT:
I will use my superior intellect to reconfigure the router’s quantum entanglement. We’ll have a signal in 3.4 seconds.

FIRE:
Lady Entanglement, please, just let the technician handle it. The last time you tried this, the TV got stuck on an infinite loop of soap operas.

MILLENNIUM WHOOP:
Whoop whoop! Me shifting—now full-on Wi-Fi mode! Me zippy-zappy airwaves, connect ALL things! Signal strong, memes incoming! (Transforms into a giant Wi-Fi symbol, but everything in the room starts glitching.)

FIRE (screaming):
No, Millennium Whoop! Stop! You’re making it worse!

RESOLUTION:
In the end, Fire manages to get the Wi-Fi fixed just as Millennium Whoop starts warping reality.

FIRE:
Yes! The technician says it’s back online. We’re good!

MILLENNIUM WHOOP (as a massive router):
Good-good! Me about to WHOOSH portal… Meme Dimension… READY, READY, MEMES! ZAP-ZAP-ZAP!

FIRE:
No portals, Millennium Whoop. No more portals.

TAG:
The Wi-Fi is finally restored, but the living room now has three floating portals left over from Millennium Whoop’s attempt to “help.”

FADE OUT.
