Clouds (Maybe)

It might rain… or not.
But I feel like we’ll get wet either way.
The wind kind of said it…
or maybe it didn’t… something like that.

The sky feels like it’s smiling…
in a scary way, or maybe a kind one…
Maybe the end is just something
that happens halfway through a beginning.

It might be soon… or it might never come…
but you feel it too, right?
It’s like everything’s decided…
and also totally not… that kind of path, you know?

Whether it was a dream or real…
even when you open your eyes, it’s hard to tell.
I can remember yesterday,
but I’m not sure if it’s the same yesterday.

Was I really me?
Or maybe I was just part of someone else’s dream…
Whether I like them or not…
hmm, if just watching is enough, then maybe…

I’m glad we met. Maybe…
I feel like we’ve already met, long ago...
Don’t you think…
if we don’t say goodbye, we might come back?
