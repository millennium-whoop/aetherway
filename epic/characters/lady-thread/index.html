Lady Thread

She’s so good with hair that people often call on her to find their thread of thought. If you’ve lost yours, here’s a little prayer to help you find it:

Lady Thread, goddess of memory and recollection. I lost my thoughts in my head’s tangled mess. Please help me find those loose ends.

PS: Your hair is the prettiest of all Ladies.

This last part is the most important. You see, when dealing with deities, it’s important to suck up to them.
