Lady Herself

Lady Nobody admired Lady Somebody and wished to become like her. She used the Dreamer’s Mirror to see herself as Lady Somebody, but instead of a reflection, the mirror showed nothing. Overwhelmed by this emptiness, Lady Nobody finally accepted her own identity. As she did, the mirror began to shine brightly. When the light faded, it revealed Lady Herself, her true self. This moment marked her acceptance of who she truly was.
