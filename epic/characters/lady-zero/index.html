Lady Zero

Lady Zero was drawn to a mysterious cave, but each time she approached, she was met with mocking laughter from within. Despite her curiosity, fear held her back. One day, summoning her courage, Lady Zero laughed defiantly at the cave and ventured inside. After exploring its wonders, she returned and noticed a small, child-sized footprint facing the opposite direction—her own, but smaller. To her surprise, instead of laughter, she heard applause coming from the cave.
