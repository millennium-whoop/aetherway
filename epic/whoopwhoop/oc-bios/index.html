What's the deal with OC bios?!

You open a character sheet—boom! 73 bullet points. Name, height, MBTI, trauma count, bubble tea order, and three quotes from a prophecy no one asked for. What is this, a dating app?

And they’re always “chaotic neutral Scorpio INFJs” who drink way too much coffee. Because nothing screams deep character like astrology, personality tests, and caffeine addiction.

Trauma count? Seventeen. Before age 15. What is this, Grey’s Anatomy?

They’ve got two eye colors, a scar with a tragic backstory, and a magic sword that “chooses its wielder.” Okay, Excalibur, take a seat.

Alignment? “Morally gray.” Translation: edgy, but indecisive.

They’re an orphan—with deep parental issues. How does that work?

Fluent in five languages—none of them real. But ask if they like dogs or cats? Total mystery. Music? “Too personal.”

And why do they always have just one friend and a thousand enemies? You’d think they’d be better at networking after all those betrayals.

Zoom-zoom thoughts? Fizzle-dazzle questions? Sync up!
