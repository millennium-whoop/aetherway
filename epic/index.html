The Epic of the Hive People

The Epic of the Hive People is set in a dimension where demon-like beings live in a stratified, hive-like society.

It is an ever-evolving collection of self-contained stories and illustrations, presented in a nonlinear, modular narrative that weaves into a larger, cohesive arc. The collection is roughly organized in chronological order based on in-story events rather than the order of creation. A major upcoming event serves as a turning point, dividing the story into two parts, with Part 2 (The End of the Beginning) unfolding as a direct consequence of what happens in Part 1 (The Beginning of the End).
