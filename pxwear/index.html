Sprites

It should work with any sprites, really, but I especially love how GBC sprites look with this technique! Their smaller, simpler designs and bold colors with high contrast make them stand out beautifully. NES and NGP sprites also look great! If you prefer the more colorful sprites like those from the SNES, MD, GBA, and later consoles, there are some examples below!

Gaps

The grid, or gaps between pixels are fully transparent perfectly blending with shirt’s colors! It’s like your chest is a LCD matrix display!

Want something subtler? Increase the gap between pixels for a sleek, more minimalist effect.

Custom Shapes!

Pixels can be shaped like squares, circles, hearts, sparkles, crosses, stars or any custom shape!

Portraits and Cutscenes

Photos
